Welcome to the Alabama Mayflower Society

The Alabama Society of Mayflower Descendants extends a warm welcome to all those interested in our heritage and endeavors. We are a community bound by the extraordinary journey of our Pilgrim ancestors aboard the Mayflower in 1620.

Our society serves to perpetuate the indomitable spirit of these brave individuals. Their signing of The Mayflower Compact laid the foundation of self-governance in America.
